Palantír to be sold to highest bidder

A palantír is coming up for auction in an auction house in Angband. A palantír is a "Seeing Stone"; when one looks into it, it can communicate with other Stones. People of great power can manipulate the palantírs to see virtually any part of Middle-earth. Gandalf the Grey has expressed great concern that the palantír should fall into the wrong hands and has set up an account for PayPal donations to raise funds for buying the palantír.
