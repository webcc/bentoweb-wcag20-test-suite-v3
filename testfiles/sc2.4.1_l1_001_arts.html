Premiere of “la sonate de Vinteuil”

Tomorrow, Amélie Thary and Alain Poulain will perform the world premiere Vinteuil's first sonata in Minas Tirith. Contrary to popular belief, this sonata, which is well known for being mentioned in Marcel Proust's novel A la recherche du temps perdu (In Seach of Lost Time), is a real composition, but it was only recently rediscovered in the archives of the Bibliothèque Mazarine.
