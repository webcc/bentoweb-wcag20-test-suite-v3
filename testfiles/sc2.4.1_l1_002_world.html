Frodo Lives!

In the last few years, there were persistent rumours that Frodo died bored of the rings. However, our reporter Christopher Neilkot says he discovered that Frodo now lives in the South of France and uses an alias when visiting Middle-earth. He also keeps wild boar in his garden to scare off overenthusiastic fans.

Lion Eats Grey Witch

Yesterday, the grey witch was eaten by her pet lion. Neighbours say they heard an argument between the witch and the lion at lunch time. Apparently, the witch was chiding the lion for putting too much gravy on his lamb chops and threatened to turn him into a wardrobe. The lion, who was tired of the endless arguments about his high cholesterol levels, had his fill of the witch and ate her.
